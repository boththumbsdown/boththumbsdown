MANIFESTO

EVERYTHING SHOULD BE INTERESTING.
MOST THINGS SHOULD BE CLEAR.
SOMETHING SHOULD RAISE A QUESTION.
WORK IS A FLINT. THE VIEWER HAS HIS/HER OWN FIREWOOD.
IF POSSIBLE THE WORK SHOULD BE SIMPLE.
METAPHORS ARE SEEDS OF MISUNDERSTANDING, BUT IF LAND AT EXACT POINT IT CAN BE THE MOST POWERFUL WEAPON.
RISK TO BE MISUNDERSTOOD. AT LEAST IN WORKS.
NOT EVERYONE'S GOING TO LIKE EVERYTHING. I DON'T LIKE EVERYONE ANYWAYS.
DON'T TRY TO IMPRESS ANYONE. IN THAT SENSE, CREATE EVERYTHING FOR MYSELF.
CAUSED THEN EXPERIENCED. ONLY THEN MAYBE EXPLAINED OR MAYBE NOT.
BETTER HATED THAN BEING BLEND AND TRIGGER NOTHING.
TRIGGER TRIGGER TRIGGER. THAT'S THE ONLY AND BIGGEST PURPOSE.
DO NOT TRY TO UNDERSTAND ANYTHING I DON'T.
'IF IT IS ART, IT IS NOT FOR ALL, AND IF IT IS FOR ALL, IT IS NOT ART.' ARNOLD SCHOENBERG. COOL.
EVERY INTERESTING THING WORTH INVESTIGATING HAS PARADOX IN IT. IN OTHER WORD, PARADOX MAKES THINGS INTERESTING.
EVERYTHING IS SUBJECTIVE. HOWEVER, I SHOULD DRAW A LINE SOMEWHERE AT ONE POINT. DON'T DELAY. MAKE MY OWN SKETCH.
THERE IS NO RIGHT OR WRONG. HOWEVER I NEED TO BE CONSISTENT.
DETACH MYSELF FROM MY WORK. WHAT MY WORK SAYS DOESN'T NECESSARILY MEAN I TRULY BELEIVE IN IT.
QUESTION EVERYTHING.
FAIL AND DO OTHER THINGS.
DON'T THINK WHILE MAKING.
